How It Works

Find What You've Lost in 3 Simple Steps

Our streamlined process makes it easy for the university community to report and recover lost belongings.

Step 1

Report Your Item

Describe the item you lost or found with details like location, date, category, and an optional photo.

Step 2

Match & Connect

Our system matches lost and found reports. Get notified when a potential match is found on campus.

Step 3

Recover & Celebrate

Arrange a safe pick-up point on campus and get reunited with your belongings. It's that easy!
